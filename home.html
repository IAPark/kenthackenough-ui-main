ABOUT

Kent Hack Enough, hacKSU's annual hackathon, brings the passion and innovation behind hackathons to the Midwest. With over 250 participants, Kent Hack Enough will be the largest hackathon in Ohio. Top prizes will include cash and exposure to top companies. Admission is completely free for students, and travel costs will be reimbursed.

RULES

In order to participate you must be a undergraduate or graduate student at a college or university. Contact us if you're a high school student wanting to participate. Projects must be started from scratch, but the use of 3rd party libraries and APIs are allowed. Team sizes are limited to 4 people. We aspire to be welcoming to everyone at KHE, and we adhere to the MLH Code of Conduct.

TRAVEL

If you are traveling from outside of Kent, we will reimburse travel costs, so there really is no excuse you kent attend! Contact us for more information.

SPONSORS

CONTACT

If you have any questions, concerns, or problems, please do not hesitate to contact us. One of our organizers will get back to you as soon as humanly possible.
